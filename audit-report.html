Main Improvement Areas

The site works, but the experience can be clearer.

The main opportunity is improving structure, presentation, organization, and long-term update flow so the site feels current and easier to manage.

Structure

Multiple H1 headings

The homepage has three H1 headings. This is not an emergency, but a polished site should usually have one clear primary H1.

SEO cleanup
Members

The member directory could likely be organized more clearly with business cards, categories, contact details, links, and social paths.
