Executive Overview

What matters

The website has a working base. It loads, has contact information, has social links, uses SSL, and has no broken/problem links from the scan. The weak point is presentation. A visitor should be able to quickly understand what the Chamber does, find events, find members, and know how to join or contact the Chamber without digging.

Best path: keep the useful content, simplify the page structure, make members and events easier to browse, and create a maintenance setup for recurring updates.
